PARACETAMOL (Brand names: Crocin, Dolo 650, Calpol, Metacin)
========================
Kya hai: Bukhar aur dard ki sabse aam aur safe dawai.

Kab lein: Bukhar, sir dard, dant dard, body ache mein.

Kaise lein (Dose):
- Adult (18+): 500mg se 1000mg (1-2 tablet) har 4-6 ghante mein. Din mein 4 baar se zyada nahi.
- Bachcha (6-12 saal): 250mg (half tablet) har 4-6 ghante.
- Chhota bacha: Doctor se poochh ke lein.

Kab nahi lein: Liver ki bimari mein. Ek din mein 4 baar se zyada nahi.

Side effects: Aam taur par safe hai. Zyada lene par liver ko nuksan.

ORS (ORAL REHYDRATION SALTS - Electral, Pedialyte)
========================
Kya hai: Dast aur ulti mein shareer ka paani aur namak wapas karne ki sabse zaroori cheez.

Kab lein: Dast, ulti, zyada pasina, dhoop mein zyada rehne ke baad.

Kaise banayein: 1 ORS packet ko 1 litre saaf paani mein ghol lein.

Dose:
- Adult: Har dast ke baad 200-400ml (1-2 glass)
- Bacha: Har dast ke baad 100-200ml. Chhote ghooont mein pilayen.

Ghar par ORS: 1 litre paani + 6 chhoti chammach cheeni + आधी chhoti chammach namak.

Zaroori: Dawai nahi — yeh shareer ka paani aur salt wapas karta hai. Dast mein sabse pehle yahi piyen.

AMOXICILLIN (Brand: Amoxil, Novamox, Mox)
========================
Kya hai: Antibiotics — bacterial infection (bacterial) ke liye.

Kab lein: Gale ki infection, ear infection, chest infection, UrinaryTract Infection. Sirf doctor ke kehne par.

Kaise lein: 250mg ya 500mg — din mein 3 baar. Khane ke saath lein. Poora course complete karein (7-10 din).

ZAROORI: Beech mein band mat karein — infection wapas aa sakti hai aur dawai kaam karna band kar deti hai.

Kab nahi lein: Penicillin se allergy ho to. Viral infection (cold, flu) mein antibiotics kaam nahi karte.

Side effects: Pet mein dard, dast, ulti. Allergy ho to — chehre par sujan, saas lene mein takleef — TURANT doctor.

---

METFORMIN (Brand: Glycomet, Glucophage, Obimet)
========================
Kya hai: Type 2 diabetes (sugar) ki dawai.

Kab lein: Sirf doctor ke likhe hone par. Blood sugar kam karne ke liye.

Kaise lein: Khane ke saath ya khane ke turant baad lein. Khali pet nahi.

Dose: Doctor ke bataye anusaar. Khud dose mat badhaayen.

Zaroori: Regular khana na khaane par ya zyada exercise karne par blood sugar gir sakta hai (hypoglycemia). Cheeni ka tukda ya juice rakhein paas mein.

Side effects: Pet mein dard, ulti, dast — start mein hota hai, theek ho jaata hai.

Kab band karein: X-ray se pehle, operation se pehle, doctor se zaroor bataayen.

---

IRON TABLETS (Ferrous Sulphate / Ferrous Fumarate)
========================
Kya hai: Khoon ki kami (anemia) ki dawai.

Kab lein: Khoon ki kami mein. Pregnancy mein doctors zaroor likhte hain. Sarkari PHC mein FREE milti hai.

Kaise lein: Nimbu paani ya orange juice ke saath lein — iron zyada absorb hota hai. Khali pet ya dopahar mein lein.

Kab nahi lein: Saath mein doodh, chai, ya calcium tablets mat lein — iron absorb nahi hota.

Side effects: Potty kaali ho sakti hai (normal hai). Kabj ho sakta hai — paani zyada piyen. Pet mein thodi takleef.

---

ZINC TABLETS
========================
Kya hai: Dast mein baachon ke liye zaroori supplement.

Kab lein: 5 saal se chhote bachon mein dast ke saath ORS ke saath zaroor dein.

Dose: 20mg (1 tablet) din mein ek baar, 14 din tak.

Faida: Dast jaldi theek hota hai. Agle 3 mahine tak dast hone ki sambhavna kam hoti hai.

Sarkari PHC mein FREE milta hai.

---

CETIRIZINE (Brand: Cetzine, Alerid, Zyrtec)
========================
Kya hai: Allergy aur khansi-naak behne ki dawai. Antihistamine.

Kab lein: Naak behna, aankh mein kharish, allergy se chheenk, jild par kharish.

Kaise lein: 10mg — raat ko sone se pehle (neend aa sakti hai). Adult ke liye.

Side effects: Neend, munh sookha lagna.

---

ANTACID (Gelusil, Digene, ENO)
========================
Kya hai: Pet mein jalan aur acid ke liye. Antacid.

Kab lein: Pet mein jalan (acidity), seene mein jalan, khatti dakar.

Kaise lein: Khane ke 1 ghante baad. Doodh ya paani ke saath.

Zaroori: Zyada din tak mat lein — doctor se milein agar problem 2 hafte se zyada ho.

---

OMEPRAZOLE (Brand: Omez, Ocid, Prilosec)
========================
Kya hai: Acidity aur stomach ulcer ki dawai. PPI (Proton Pump Inhibitor).

Kab lein: Baar baar acid, ulcer, GERD. Doctor ke likhe anusaar.

Kaise lein: Khane se 30 min pehle, subah. Khali pet lein.

---

AZITHROMYCIN (Brand: Zithromax, Azithral)
========================
Kya hai: Antibiotic — sirf doctor ke prescription par lein.

Kab lein: Chest infection, typhoid, kuch skin infections. Doctor ki salah par.

Kaise lein: 500mg ek baar roz, 3-5 din. Khana ke saath ya bina khane ke — liya ja sakta hai.

ZAROORI: Poora course lein — beech mein band mat karein.

---

DEXAMETHASONE / BETAMETHASONE (Steroids)
========================
Kya hai: Inflammation aur allergy ki tez dawai. Steroid.

WARNING: Kabhi bhi khud se shuru mat karein. Sirf doctor ke likhe par. Beech mein achanak band mat karein — dangerous ho sakta hai.

Long term use se: Sugar badh sakti hai, haddiyan kamzor ho sakti hain.

---

VITAMIN D3 (Cholecalciferol)
========================
Kya hai: Vitamin D ki kami ke liye. India mein bahut aam kami hai.

Lakshan kami ke: Haddiyon aur muscles mein dard, thakan, bacho mein rickets (tedhi haddiyan).

Kaise lein: Doctor ke bataye anusaar. Aam taur par weekly ya monthly dose. Khane ke saath lein (fat mein ghulta hai).
