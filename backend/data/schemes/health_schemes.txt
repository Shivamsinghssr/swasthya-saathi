AYUSHMAN BHARAT - PRADHAN MANTRI JAN AROGYA YOJANA (PMJAY)
========================
Kya hai: Bharat sarkar ki sabse badi health insurance yojana. 5 lakh rupaye tak ka muft ilaaj.

Kise milega (Eligibility):
- SECC 2011 data mein jo parivaar hain
- Antyodaya Anna Yojana (AAY) card wale
- BPL card wale (zyada tar)
- Asanghit karmchari aur unke parivaar

Faida: 5 lakh rupaye tak muft ilaaj. Cancer, heart surgery, kidney, liver jaise bade ilaaj shamil hain. 1400 se zyada bimariyan cover hain.

Kaise milega: Apne gram sabha ya CSC (Common Service Centre) mein jayen. Ration card aur Aadhar card lekar.

Kahan use karein: Empanelled sarkari aur private aspatal mein. Aarogyamitra se milein aspatal mein.

Helpline: 14555 ya 1800-111-565 (free call)

---

MUKHYAMANTRI JAN AROGYA YOJANA (UP - UTTAR PRADESH)
========================
Kya hai: Uttar Pradesh sarkar ki health yojana — PMJAY ke saath aur zyada faida.

Kise milega: UP ke woh parivaar jo PMJAY mein cover nahi hain lekin garib hain. SPY (State Priority Households).

Faida: 5 lakh rupaye tak muft ilaaj. PMJAY se zyada aspatal mein kaam karta hai UP mein.

Kaise karein apply: Jila aspatal ya CHC mein jayen. Aadhar aur ration card saath lein.

---

JANANI SURAKSHA YOJANA (JSY) - PREGNANT WOMEN
========================
Kya hai: Prasav (delivery) ke liye sarkari sahayata. Mahilaon ko ghar pe delivery ki jagah aspatal mein delivery karne ke liye protsahan.

Faida:
- Rural areas: Rs. 1400 sahayata (BPL mahila ko aspatal mein delivery par)
- Urban areas: Rs. 1000
- ASHA worker ko bhi incentive milta hai aapko aspatal le jaane ke liye

Saath mein milega: Muft prasav. Muft davaiyan. Muft khaan paan aspatal mein.

Kaise lein: Apni ASHA worker se sampark karein. Gram Pradhan se poochein. PHC mein jayen.

---

PRADHAN MANTRI MATRU VANDANA YOJANA (PMMVY)
========================
Kya hai: Pehle zinda bacche ke liye garbhvati aur stan-paan karane wali mahilaon ko Rs. 5000 sahayata.

Yeh paisa 3 kisht mein milta hai:
1. Pahli kist (Rs. 1000): Pehli baar pregnancy register karwane par
2. Doosri kist (Rs. 2000): Pehla antenatal checkup hone par
3. Teesri kist (Rs. 2000): Baccha hone aur pehle tikakaran ke baad

Kaise karein: Anganwadi ya PHC mein registration karwayen.

---

RASHTRIYA BAL SWASTHYA KARYAKRAM (RBSK) - CHILDREN HEALTH
========================
Kya hai: 0-18 saal ke bachon ki muft health jaanch aur ilaaj.

Kya cover hota hai:
- Janam se hi bimariyan (birth defects)
- Poshan ki kami
- Behriapan (deafness), andhapan (blindness)
- Seekhne mein takleef (learning disability)
- Dil ki bimari (heart diseases)

Kaise: Sarkari school aur anganwadi mein RBSK team aati hai. Aspatal mein bhi free jaanch hoti hai.

---

NATIONAL HEALTH MISSION (NHM) - MUFT SEVAYEIN
========================
Yeh sarkari PHC aur CHC mein FREE milta hai:
- OPD mein doctor se milna — FREE
- Malaria, TB, HIV ka ilaj — FREE
- Janani Suraksha Yojana ke under prasav — FREE
- Tikakaran (Vaccination) — FREE
- Family Planning dawaiyan — FREE
- Iron aur folic acid tablets — FREE (pregnant mahilaon ke liye)
- ORS packets — FREE
- Contraceptives — FREE

Yaad rakhein: Sarkari PHC aur CHC mein bahut kuch free hai — paise ke wajah se ilaaj mat rokein.

MUKHYAMANTRI KANYA SUMANGALA YOJANA (UP)
========================
Kya hai: UP mein beti ke janm se lekar padhai tak sahayata (Rs. 15,000 total).

Faida (6 kisht mein):
1. Janm par: Rs. 2000
2. Tikakaran hone par (1 saal mein): Rs. 1000
3. Class 1 mein: Rs. 2000
4. Class 6 mein: Rs. 2000
5. Class 9 mein: Rs. 3000
6. Graduation ya 2 saal diploma: Rs. 5000

Eligibility: UP ke niwasi. Parivaar ki saalik aamdani Rs. 3 lakh se kam. Ek parivaar mein maximum 2 betiyaan.

Apply: mksy.up.gov.in ya CSC center par.

CHIEF MINISTER HEALTH INSURANCE (BIHAR - MUKHYAMANTRI SWASTHYA BIMA YOJANA)
========================
Kya hai: Bihar sarkar ki health insurance yojana — poor families ke liye.

Faida: Rs. 5 lakh tak muft ilaaj empanelled aspiratals mein.

Kaise: Jila aspatal ya CSC center mein jayen. Ration card aur Aadhar saath lein.

---

ASHA WORKER SE MILEIN
========================
ASHA (Accredited Social Health Activist) aapke gaon ki health worker hain.

Woh IN SAB mein madad karti hain — MUFT:
- Aspatal le jaana (Janani Suraksha ke liye)
- JSY ki kist dilwana
- Tikakaran karwana
- PHC mein registration
- Ayushman card banwana
- Poshan ki jaankari

Yeh yaad rakhein: ASHA worker ko koi paisa mat dein unke sarkari kaam ke liye — yeh FREE service hai.
