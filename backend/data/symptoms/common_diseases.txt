BUKHAR (FEVER / TAPI)
========================
Lakshan (Symptoms): Sharer ka temperature 100°F (37.8°C) ya zyada ho jaana, kaanpna (thanda lagana), sir dard, thakan, bhookh na lagna.

Kya karna chahiye (Home Care):
- Paracetamol 500mg lein (adult ke liye)
- Zyada se zyada paani, nariyal paani, ORS piyen
-濕 kapda mathe pe rakhein
- Zyada kapdein mat pahnen

Doctor ke paas kab jayen:
- Bukhar 103°F (39.4°C) se zyada ho
- 3 din se zyada rahe
- Bacha bahut zyada thaka hua ho
- Saas lene mein takleef ho

Sambhavit karan (Possible causes): Malaria, Typhoid, Dengue, viral infection, TB

---

MALARIA
========================
Lakshan: Thanda lagna phir zyada garmi, kaanpna, tez bukhar, sir dard, ulti. Lakshan 2-3 din mein cycle mein aate hain.

Khatre ki nishaniyan (Danger signs): Behoshi, khoon ki ulti, peele aankhein, bahut kamzori — TURANT aspatal jayen.

Ilaj (Treatment): Malaria ki jaanch (blood test) zaroor karwayen. Sarkari PHC mein free mein hoti hai. Doctor se dawai lein — khud se dawai mat lein.

Bachaao (Prevention): Machchar dani (mosquito net) ka use karein. Ghar ke aas paas paani na jamne dein. Machchar repellent lagayen.

---

TYPHOID (MOTIYABIND BUKHAR / MAIYAD BUKHAR)
========================
Lakshan: Dheere-dheere badhta bukhar, pet dard, dast ya kabj, sir dard, thakan, bhookh na lagna. Bukhar aam taur par sham ko zyada hota hai.

Khatre ki nishaniyan: Pet mein tez dard, khoon ke dast, behoshi — TURANT doctor ke paas jayen.

Jaanch: Blood test (Widal test) ya stool test se pata chalta hai.

Ilaj: Doctor ki dawai poori karein. Wifaq par mat khatam karein — infection wapas aa sakti hai.

Khaan-paan: Halka khana — khichdi, dahi, dal ka paani. Bahar ka khana bilkul mat khaayen.

---

DENGUE
========================
Lakshan: Tez bukhar (achanak), sar dard, aankhon ke peeche dard, haddiyon mein dard (haddiyaan todhne wala bukhar), jild par laal daane, ulti.

Khatre ki nishaniyan (Emergency): Naak ya mandon se khoon, pet mein tez dard, thakan bahut zyada, bar bar ulti — 108 call karein ya TURANT aspatal jayen.

Ghar par kya karein: ORS piyen, paracetamol lein (aspirin/ibuprofen bilkul mat lein — khoon nikalna badh sakta hai). Doctor se zaroor milein.

Koi khaas dawai nahi — safed blood cells (platelets) badhane ke liye zyada paani aur rest zaroori hai.

---

DAST / LOOSE MOTIONS / DIARRHEA
========================
Lakshan: Baara bar paani jaisi potty jaana, pet mein marodh, kabhi kabhi bukhar.

Sabse bada khatre: Paani ki kami (dehydration) — khaaskar bachon mein.

Dehydration ki nishani: Munh sookha lagna, aankhein andar dhans jaana, peeshab kam aana, ro na paana (bacha).

Kya karein: ORS (Oral Rehydration Salt) piyen — ek packet ek litre paani mein. Har dast ke baad ek glass ORS. Khana band mat karein — halka khana khaten rahein.

Doctor ke paas kab: Khoon ke dast, 2 din mein theek na ho, bacha bilkul kuchh na piye.

---

KHANSI AUR THANDA / COUGH AND COLD
========================
Lakshan: Naak behna, gala kharaab, khansi, hafte bhar tak halka bukhar.

Ghar par ilaj: Garam paani mein namak daalkar gargle karein. Adrak, tulsi, shahad wali chai piyen. Paani zyada piyen. Thanda paani aur ice cream se parhez karein.

Doctor ke paas kab: Khansi 2 hafte se zyada rahe, saas lene mein awaaz aaye (wheeze), tez bukhar ho, bacha kuch bhi khana-peena band kare.

---

TB (TUBERCULOSIS / KSHAY ROG)
========================
Lakshan: 2 hafte se zyada khansi, khoon wali khansi, raat ko pasina aana, wajan kam hona, bhookh na lagna, halka bukhar sham ko.

Zaroori baat: TB bilkul theek ho sakti hai — sarkari aspatal mein MUFT ilaj milta hai. Dawai poori karein — 6 mahine. Beech mein band mat karein.

Jaanch: Sputum test (thook ki jaanch) — bilkul free sarkari PHC mein.

---

ANEMIA (KHOON KI KAMI)
========================
Lakshan: Thakan, kamzori, chhoti saans, chehra paala ho jaana, haath-nakhoon safed/paale hona, chakkar aana.

Kaaran: Loh (iron) ki kami — khaaskar mahilaon aur bachon mein. Zyada khoon nikalna. Poshan ki kami.

Ghar par: Hara saag (palak, methi), chana, daal, anda, khejoor khaayen. Nimbu ke saath iron wali cheezein khaayen (Vitamin C se iron absorb hota hai).

Doctor se: Iron tablet lein — sarkari PHC mein free milti hai. Pregnancy mein zaroor lein.

---

PEELI AANKHEIN / JAUNDICE (PILIYA)
========================
Lakshan: Aankhein aur jild peeli ho jaana, peela peeshab, thakan, pet dard, bhookh na lagna.

Khatre ki nishani: Tez bukhar ke saath peeli aankhein — TURANT aspatal jayen.

Ghar par: Paani bahut zyada piyen. Ganna juice, nariyal paani piyen. Tel-masale se parhez.

Doctor se: Blood test zaroor karwayen. Liver ki jaanch zaroori hai.

---

DIABETES (MADHUMEH / SUGAR KI BIMARI)
========================
Lakshan: Baar baar pyaas lagna, baar baar peeshab, thakan, aankhein dhundhli, zakham dheere theeek hona.

Ghar par kya karein: Cheeni, meetha, maida kam khaayen. Chawal, aloo kam karein. Rozmarra vyayam karein.

Doctor se: Blood test karwayen. Dawai poori karein — band mat karein.

Khatre ki nishani: Paon sunn ho jaana, zakham na bharna — turant doctor se milein.
